## January 2025: ECMA-427 Approval for PURL

We are excited to announce the ISO fast-track approval of **ECMA-427** for the **Package URL (PURL)** specification!

*   **Date**: January 2025
*   **Details**: The Package URL (PURL) standard, convened by Philippe Ombredanne of AboutCode, has reached a major milestone with its approval as an ECMA standard. This validates PURL as the de facto standard for software package identification across the software supply chain.
